JACK CHANG FOR THE FIRST TIME IN POLAND! // PANDEMONIUM

For the first time in Poland - DJ Jack Chang!

With over twenty years of experience behind the console, Jack Chang is not only a world-class star, but also a DJ with a world-class reputation. So far, he has performed in 25 countries and 60 cities around the world from Honolulu to Sydney. He's a bit torn between the more fetish scene and the more mainstream one, and his well-established DJ stance continues across the spectrum of gay universe events, with success at both ends of the spectrum.

Jack is known in Europe in the fetish environment, through his cooperation with events such as Folsom Europe from its beginnings in 2004 as a street event and the closing event, PiG Berlin, Amsterdam Leather Pride events (since 2005) as well as virtually every other leather event on the continent, from "Into the Tank" in Madrid, through the Hamburg Leather Weekend, to smaller events at clubs such as The Hoist and Eagle in London. What's more, he was the youngest resident of clubs such as Suzie Kreuger's FIST club (now HardOn), The Boots (Antwerp) and SLM (Copenhagen). He still performs (even after 23 years) at Maspalomas Fetish Week and in Darklands (Antwerp).

In 2008, Jack's fame spread across the Atlantic, where he performed on both the East and West Coast of the US at Los Angeles Leather Pride, Palm Springs Leather Pride, The Eagle (New York), Eagle Atlanta, and more to the north - Canada in Eagle Toronto, Ruff Vancouver, or further south - in Mexico for the Manifesto, or in Brazil at The Original Brazilian Pool Party and its sister party, Revolution.

His stubbornness and hard work earned him recognition in the community by becoming the Best Fetish DJ twice (as the first DJ in the world), receiving awards from the X-Awards in 2013 and 2017.

He is a classically educated musician (piano, violin, music theory - 11 schools), and his success among the audience, apart from concerts, is due to his versatility as a remixer, producer, working for independent labels from Europe, North and South America. He produces songs in several languages, and his fame is growing thanks to his work in the studio as well as live performances at clubs and parties around the world.

He is currently hosting a streamed program "Virtual World Tour" in cooperation with existing and new partners. He produces and remixes for 6 labels in a studio in Frankfurt am Main.
