Pełech & Horna Duo

A combination of original arrangements in the style of samba, bossa-new, tango and improvised smooth-jazz atmosphere. It is a wealth of beautiful melodies, harmony and rhythm. The band's program includes, among others, famous compositions by Pat Metheny, Ralph Towner, Keith Jarrett, Astor Piazzolla, Stanley Myers, Isaac Albeniz, Francisco Tarrega, as well as original arrangements of works by Celso Machado and Aziza Mustafa Zadeh.

Krzysztof Pełech is an outstanding musician from Wrocław, three times selected as the best Polish classical guitarist by the "Gitara i Bas" magazine. He is the Artistic Director of the Wrocław Guitar Festival Gitara + / Guitar Masters and the Summer Guitar Course in Krzyżowa. The artist performs about 60 concerts a year and has 16 albums to his credit. Since 2005, he has been teaching guitar at the Academy of Music in Bydgoszcz, where he obtained the title of doctor of arts. Krzysztof Pełech has collaborated with leading Polish orchestras, as well as with the Berlin Festival Orchestra. The artist has performed in almost all European countries, in the USA, Brazil, South Africa, as well as in Egypt, Singapore, Indonesia, Malaysia, Argentina and Chile. He is also often invited to collaborate with outstanding composers and guitarists, such as: Jorge Morel, Jorge Cardoso,

Robert Horna is a laureate of many national and international guitar competitions. He actively gives concerts in Poland, Switzerland, Romania, Moldova, Germany, Belgium, the Netherlands and South America. Guests with recitals at many prestigious music festivals in Poland and abroad, during which he is regularly invited to conduct master classes. Robert Horna is a versatile artist - he performs in chamber ensembles, concerts with orchestras and as part of solo recitals. The guitarist is also the author of three solo albums released by CD Accord Music Edition.
