EDUreakcJA 2.0 action - anti-discrimination training

We are starting the Action EDUreakacJA 2.0 series!

We invite you to take part in anti-discrimination trainings dedicated to the student community, educators and educators as well as all those who are close to equality education.

Thanks to the training you will learn what discrimination is and how it arises. We will cover the topic of sexual harassment, sexual harassment and consent. We will talk about how to counteract inequalities in public space, at universities and on the Internet.

The trainings are part of the project, which is a continuation of the pilot project Action EDUrekacjJa co-created by the Scientific Association of Equality Education "Emancipation". The pilot showed how important and necessary such actions are. 60 students have received anti-discrimination training. Now it's time for more!

According to the report of the Human Rights Defender "The experience of harassment among female and male students", it appears that 40.7% of students have experienced harassment, and the perpetrator / perpetrator of every third such situation was a researcher / former researcher. From the preliminary analyzes of the study conducted by the University of Poznań Adam Mickiewicz shows that every tenth person has experienced harassment.

The report “The social situation of LGBTA people in Poland. Report for 2019-2020 ”indicates that 53% of non-heteronormative people have experienced hate crimes. Discrimination also affects many other social groups - women, migrants and migrants, seniors and seniors, people of religions other than Catholicism and other less dominant groups.

Counteracting and noticing these negative phenomena is crucial in actions for equality and social justice.

We invite you to change reality with us! ✊?
